In Memory of my beloved father Michael Katzav 🕯







About him ðŸ‘‘

My father, Michael Katzav, was born in Israel on 17.12.1968 - 16.03.2023.
He was married to my mother Sigal Katzav and was the father of three children: Tomer, Rotem, and Yuval.
Father's field of business was signage, and he had an independent business in Be'er Sheva where he spent all his time working hard.
His hobbies were riding motorcycles and football, which he passed on to me and my brothers, and we liked to spend most of our time together.
Father was the source of my support in particular and of the whole family in general. He taught us about modesty, respect for others, hard work, and perseverance. Father passed away in the last year, and the longing for him only grows.
I will dedicate every step of my life to my father and continue my path with the help of all the tools he taught me over the years.

Q&A Answers

1. Differences between Web1, Web2, and Web3:

Web1: Refers to the early stage of the internet with static websites providing basic information.
Web2: Represents the interactive web era with dynamic sites, user-generated content, and social platforms.
Web3: Concept of a decentralized internet with blockchain, smart contracts, and focus on privacy and security.

2. Explanation with Example:

When searching for "Side Effects of Medicines":
- Web1: Basic static information from existing websites.
- Web2: Access to a wider range of platforms and user-generated content.
- Web3: More secure and reliable data stored using blockchain, ensuring trustworthiness.
